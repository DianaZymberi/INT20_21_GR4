Fire sign

Like fire itself, fire signs tend to be passionate, dynamic, and temperamental.Fire can keep you warm, or it can do great destruction.While fire burns out quickly without fuel to keep it going, it can also regenerate its power from the ashes.A single spark can set off a forest fire.As a result, fire signs need to be nurtured and managed carefully.

About success

Fire signs are passionate, creative, and instinctual. They follow their intuition and rarely stop to weigh all of the consequences before taking action or making a decision. A Fire sign’s creativity is its best feature, driving them toward unique and exciting experiences. Your idea of success involves anything that speaks to your heart and makes you feel excited.

About love

People born under the Fire element have a unique spark in their eyes. They’re full of light, warm and can impress anyone. When in love, they’re innocent and give their everything to their partner, not to mention they tend to listen more to their heart than to their mind.
