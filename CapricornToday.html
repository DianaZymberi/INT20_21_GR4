The Empress

The Empress is all about personal growth. The power she possesses is unlimited.

Take a look at the Empress' scepter. She wields it with a force that's gentle but firm.
She rises from green foliage below, with a crown lined with flowers, attracting a
bee—all of which is symbolic of her innate connection with nature. The symbol of
Venus hovers above her as a portrayal of divine femininity. The Empress embraces
all aspects of her being—physical, sexual, emotional—with a voracious appetite
that holds no room for fear. If you stop and listen, you can hear her whispering,
"Embrace your power," directly into your soul.
