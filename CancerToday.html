Card of the day

The Emperor

Think of someone you admire. If that person is responsible, decisive,
and clear in their thinking and action, they have much in common with the Emperor.
That person may not be a nurturer by nature, but they make up for it with their
ability to plan, structure, and delegate. And the world is better for it.

Of course, the Emperor's rigidity may not always be in order.
While his scepter and stern eye may gleam as though he has everything under control,
the confining foliage beneath him indicates that too much structure can rob us of
our lust for life.
