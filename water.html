Water sign

Intuitive, emotional and ultra-sensitive, water signs can be as mysterious as the ocean itself. Like water, they can be refreshing, or they can drown you in their depths. These signs often have intense dreams and borderline-psychic intuition. Security is important to them—after all, water needs a container, or it dries up and disappears.

About success

Water signs are deeply connected to their emotions, their intuition, and their creativity. Water signs feel their way through life, following their heart in most situations. Success for a Water sign means being able to do what feels right in the moment. That is the destiny of the Water signs — to fill the planet with as much love and passion as possible.

About love

If a water sign falls for you, they're usually all in. They love with all their hearts and don't hold back. That may sound intense, but being loved entirely is an experience everyone should have.
