Earth sign

Earth signs keep it real. They are the “grounded” people on the planet, the ones who bring us down to earth and remind us to start with a solid foundation. Slow and steady, these “builders” are loyal and stable, and stick by their people through hard times. On good days, they’re practical; at worst, they can be materialistic or too focused on the surface of things to dig into the depths.

About success

Known for their responsible, hard-working nature, Earth signs are almost inevitably successful. They are determined and driven enough to go after what they want, and they aren’t afraid to roll up their sleeves and get their hands dirty. But it’s important for these signs to realize that ultimate success also involves play, connection with others, and time to enjoy your hobbies.

About love

Earth sign compatibility is a complex thing – the three earth signs are all stable, gentle, tolerant folk, so you’d think that a love compatibility match would be easy with almost everyone….but actually, each earth sign has very different requirements in a relationship.
