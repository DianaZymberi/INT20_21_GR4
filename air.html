Air sign

Air signs are all about action, ideas, and motion—they are the "winds of change". When a strong gust hits you, you can’t help but move. While some within their ranks may be true-life "airheads", others are as powerful as a gravity-defying G-force. Air signs bring everyone a breath of fresh air when things start to get stale.

About success

They’re able to find quick solutions to problems or challenges, but they can be quite indecisive. And because they spend so much time in their heads, it can be hard for them to connect to their emotions!Success for an Air sign is a career, relationship, or life circumstance that allows them to connect the body to the mind.

About love

Air element natives may find it quite hard to remain committed to only one partner because they love to flirt and are innately curious about other people. They’re very good artists and communicators, not to mention charming and interesting.
