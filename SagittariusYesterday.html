Card of yesterday

The Magician

The Magician Tarot card represents someone at the beginning of their journey. The path is new, but they have no fear. They have all the tools they need.

The Magician holds out his hands. In his possession he occupies all the suits of the Minor Arcana: Cups, Swords, Pentacles, and Wands. His white cloak symbolizes his purity of character; his shirt beneath has a Mercury glyph to represent the communicative planet he rules by. The infinity symbol above his head and the snake biting its own tail, both representing never-ending cycles, point to his unlimited potential. No matter what spells or illusions the Magician creates, he does so with the confidence of someone
who knows they will succeed.
